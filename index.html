Jianjin Xu

I am an Assistant Research Scientist of Computer Science at Panzhihua University. Before that, I obtained my master's at Columbia University and bachelor's at Tsinghua University. My main research interests lie in generative modeling, 3D modeling, and deep learning interpretation. The ultimate goal of my research is to recreate an interactive virtual world.
