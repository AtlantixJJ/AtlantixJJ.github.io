Extracting Semantic Knowledge from GANs with Unsupervised Learning

1Panzhihua University
2Tsinghua University
3Chinese Academy of Sciences

We propose K-means with Linear Separability Heuristic (KLiSH) to decode semantic parts in GAN's features. The decoded semantics are fine-grained and accurate. One can use the decoded semantics for downstream segmentation and semantic image editing tasks.

Work done while working at THBI, Tsinghua as a Research Assistant.
